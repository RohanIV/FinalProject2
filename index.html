In One Piece, the bounty system is a widespread practice in the pirate-infested world. The World Government issues bounties on certain individual and pirates, based on their threat level and criminal activities. Bounties serve as monetary rewards for capturing them. The higher the bounty, the greater the perceived danger. The system encourages bounty hunters, Marines, and other pirates to pursue wanted individuals.
